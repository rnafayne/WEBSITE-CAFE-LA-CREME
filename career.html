Careers & Opportunity

Join The Team

At Café La Crème, we are always looking for passionate and dedicated individuals to join our family. If you love coffee, have a warm personality, and are eager to provide excellent customer service, we would love to hear from you!

Barista

As a Barista, you'll be responsible for crafting delicious coffee beverages and providing exceptional customer service.

Requirements:

  • Experience in coffee making preferred
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Café Manager

The Café Manager oversees daily operations, ensures high-quality customer service, and manages staff.

Requirements:

  • Previous management experience in the food industry
  • Strong leadership skills
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks

Kitchen Staff

Our Kitchen Staff prepares food items according to recipes and ensures a clean working environment.

Requirements:

  • Experience in food preparation
  • Attention to detail
  • Good organizational skills
