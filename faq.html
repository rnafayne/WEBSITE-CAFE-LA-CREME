How did you get into the coffee business?
Passion for Coffee:
Many people start in the coffee business because they have a genuine love for coffee. They appreciate its flavors, origins, and the culture surrounding it.
Barista Experience:
Working as a barista in a café or coffee shop is a common entry point. Baristas learn about coffee preparation, customer service, and the nuances of different coffee beans and brewing methods.
Coffee Roasting and Brewing Knowledge:
Learning about coffee roasting and brewing techniques through courses, workshops, or apprenticeships can lead to a career in specialty coffee.
Education and Training:
Formal education in hospitality management, culinary arts, or business administration can provide a foundation for entering the coffee industry at a managerial or operational level.

What is coffee basic info?
What is coffee? Coffee is a beverage brewed from the roasted and ground seeds of the tropical evergreen coffee plant. Coffee is one of the three most popular beverages in the world (alongside water and tea), and it is one of the most profitable international commodities.

What makes people go to a cafe?
One of the most significant reasons people enjoy going to coffee shops is the atmosphere. Coffee shops are designed to be comfortable and welcoming, with warm lighting, cozy seating, and a relaxed ambiance.
